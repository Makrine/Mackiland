History

Mackiland is one of the oldest continually inhabited countries, having been populated for 3.5 billion years (if we count micro-organisms).

Fossils of an early species of hominid have only been discovered in Mackiland. This flamboyant species of early man likely had a dramatic but boisterous attitude and is called Homo Sexualus.

Some scholars believe the ancient Greek Macedonians to have actually been Mackilandians. This would explain the success of their famous ruler, Alexander the Great.

Mackiland has historically resisted invasion, innovating the world-renowned military tactic of "hiding in the mountains and waiting til the enemy runs out of bullets to shoot."

During the global adoption of fiat money, Mackiland chose to use banana leafs over banknotes since, in the words of the president at the time, "Dude, like, hard cash be, like, coming from trees n' shit already, feel me?"

After World War II: III, the region of Macktatoria declared its independence under the dictator Macktor. She is known for her totalitarian policies, stylizing her supporters as her "fandom," and for her hardline policy against the radical anti-government movement called "Freespirito."

The nation allied itself with the USSR, but only because of a clerical error, a staffer confusing "USSR" with "USA." The government realized this in 1952, but they never officially switched their policies since "it would be too embarassing to admit our mistake," their Minister of Shame said.

Mackiland is currently a nation-state in the region of Absurdistan.
