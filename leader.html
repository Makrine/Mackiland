Our Glorious Leader



"Mackiland is brought to you in part by Dr. Pepper. 'Dr. Pepper: Always one of a kind!™'"

~Makrine

Makrine was born in a small, scenic village in the hinterlands of Country Georgia. Though interested in computer science and business, Makrine found out she could combine the two, starting an independent company building fake governmental websites for nation-simulation gaming nerds.

It was through this exposure to politics that Makrine discovered it would be more economical in the long-run to declare independence from and wage a secessionist war against Georgia than pay Georgia's business tax each year.

Makrine is renowned for being both a compassionate and adroit leader, growing the country's economy from a basket case to a frightening powerhouse. All the while, she has kept government programs to help individuals in need of assistance.

In her spare time, she tinkers with electronics or creates artwork. NEVER at the same time. She is known for her international correspondences, particularly with the leader of Czechostan.
